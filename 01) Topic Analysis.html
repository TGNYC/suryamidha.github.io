01) Topic Analysis

Topic

The United States ought to end its provision of arms to foreign insurgents

Lecture

  • Background
    • Which branch of the US?
      • Executive branch, congress also has some say and ability to block, CIA also gives arms
    • Arms
      • Weapons, excludes provisionary supplies
      • Non-lethal weapons is not included under "arms", but debateable
  • Definitions
    • Insurgents
      • defined from Merriam, or GAI from the CIA
        • There is no one definiton of insurgents, linked by struggle for resources or territory, terrorists do not want resources or territory
      • Insurgent Examples
        • Syrian rebels (Free Syrian Rebels)
        • Yemen (Houthis)
        • Turkish Coup (Second Army)
      • Not Insurgents
        • Illicit arms (black market, arms don't have to come from the US)
        • Actual countries
        • Taiwan
      • History of Sales to Insurgencies
        • Greece (help communist insurgencies) worked
        • Bay of Pigs (CIA trained Cuban guerrillas to fight Fidel Castro's troops) disaster
        • Contra (Bring down Nicaragua's Sandinista) falied
        • Afghanistan (soviet withdrawl) worked short term, but ultimately falied long term
        • Angola (sold arms)
        • Somalia (armed somalian warlords) responsible for war torn somalia
      • Spec
        • Whole resolution
        • Plans (Syria)
  • Aff Arguments
    • Rebels are unjust
      • Answer
        • Many are fighting unjust governments
    • Escalation (fight will take longer if rebels have weapons)
      • Answers
        • Escalation good (prevents rebels from dying out, force government to stop doing bad stuff, may happen regardless of arms)
        • Alt causes
    • Stolen Weapons (Syrian support weapons in black market because of Jordanian government, also which groups with what views do we support)
      • Answers
        • Already exists (Rebels/ISIS steal from gov weapon depots anyway)
        • Smart weapons (can't be used illegally, locked geographically/chronologically/gps, ex gps in missle systems)
    • Philosophical
      • Just War (fund the right groups under certain criteria, Pattinson focuses on UTIL)
        • Answers
          • Can't deal with insurgencies
          • Assist in some instances
      • Kant: Revolution is immoral (even if gov is illegetimate, they still support human rights)
        • Answers
          • Revolution is moral (Kamiske)
      • Sovereignty
        • Answers
          • Government is unjust (ok to provide if rising against unjust government)
