12) How to Beat Theory

Topic

How do you beat a theory debater?

Lecture

  • Know your judge well
    • Different judges have different preferences
    • Have a feel for how they feel about theory
    • In theory, judges intervene the most often or have the hardest time judging
    • Win these debates by prepping for the judge
  • Paradigm issues
    • Don't like having arguments about drop argument/debater
    • Debating paradigm issues correctly
    • RVI
      • Never go for it unless in the AFF case
      • If they're runnin ghteory, they must answer
      • Except RVI, Theory Spikes can end up as liabilities
    • Reasonability guidelines
      • Just cross applying warrans not good enough
      • Want to have a reasonability guideline they haven't heard before
    • Time
      • See how much time was spent on paradigm issues
      • If people read a bunch of drop the debaters in the NR, run they must be in NC
      • Hedge against predictable norms
  • Make good arguments
    • Don't read abusive arguments
      • Read solvencey advocates
        • Needs to be very credible
      • No theory spikes
      • Modify your position to exclude their grounds
        • They'll hate it
      • Advocacy debate
      • Framework
      • Disclosure
      • CX checks
        • I will tell you argument will link / what will turn
        • Modify advocacy
        • True and very respectable argument for judges
      • Make theory debaters seem as abusive as possible
    • Don't make silly arguments
      • It is unlikely that you'll win every layer of the flow
      • Theory debaters debate in a very technical way
      • Having a very strong standard is a good way to win against a theory debater
      • A theory debater will try to outtech you on every level
      • Don't get drawn to the mess of the flow
      • Go for the strongest arguments rather than everywhere on the flow
      • Turn theory technicality by focusing on an argument and predicting where they're going
      • Go for truth and big picture
  • Why do theory debaters lose
    • They beat themselves
    • Always make truer better arguments
    • Theory debaters loose when debates were a mess
    • If you're desperate, make flow a catastrophe
      • Making a lot of standards/arguments
      • Don't hold yourself to such high of argument quality
    • Look for defensive arguments
      • Most judges will be very receptivr
      • Go for quantity
      • Make a lot of weighing after everything on your counter interp
      • Theory debaters loose when things are a mess

      • The best ways to take out theory or answer it well
      • Reminder to know your judge
        • Always go for the K if left leading judge

      • Make it bulletproof
        • If they're desperate you win

      • Make sure knowledge is not exclusive or not oppressive
      • Make it more explicit
        • No reason why you shouldn't be explicit
      • If debaters are running away from an argument, emphasize it
      • At least 3 places to explicitly take out theory
      • K over theory
      • If K AFF, go for more RVIs in the NEG
